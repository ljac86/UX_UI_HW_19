Hi I'm Laura! A User Experience Designer based out of Columbus Ohio. I also hold a Bachelor’s degree in Psychology and love exploring the connection between human behavior and design.

My passion is improving the user experience to enhance daily functions in life and work. I am currently working at a small consulting company, helping to enhance the company website and internal applications.

My UX Skills

User Research

I like to speak directly with the users to gain insight on potential problems and discover the user’s needs. This step is vital before beginning any wireframing.

User Empathy

With a background in Psychology, I understand the importance of human behavior and design and strive to understand how users feel while interacting with the programs and web pages I have helped to design.

Prototyping

I use an iterative approach to wireframing and prototyping, taking frequent user feedback into account to make sure we are on the right track before it gets to development.
